
-----------------------------------------------

MIDI interface with FM and AM

LFOs:
 - rate
 - waveshape

Envelopes:
 - A/D/S/R
 - curve

FM functions:
 per operator
  ratio, amount per destination (one being external L and R)
 algorithm / output

Destinations:
 - Pitch  / L out
 - Amplitude / R out
 - DAC outputs A / B
 - trigger outputs A / B
 - operator amount
 - operator pitch

Default mono midi mapping:
 Trigger out A: gate
 Trigger out B: trigger, clock?
 CV out A: CC1
 CV out B: CC2? Aftertouch?
 L out: pitch (note + pb)
 R out: amplitude (velocity*sensitivity*volume)

Duophonic mapping:
 Trigger out A / B: gate
 CV out A / B: velocity
 Audio out L / R: pitch
 
settings:
- pitch bend range
- base note / transpose
- velocity sensitivity
- velocity curve

- clock: internal / external
- clock tempo
- clock start/stop

- pitch input: ignore pitch bend? threshold?
- midi output max message rate (for inputs)
