The transfer function of an SSM2164 deduced by experiment is approximately:
Iout=Iin*exp(-3.489*VC).

The linearized circuit has a non inverting summing control port.
You have a VCA with the transfer function. Iout=Iin*(Ictrl/Iref). Where Iref can be fixed to give Iout=k*Iin*Ictrl.
